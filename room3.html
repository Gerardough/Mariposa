The Mariposa Farm Resort is a tranquil farm, family oriented and peaceful hotel-like property that boasts 6 spacious rooms for guests to get away from the hustle and bustle of the city and embrace nature and all of it’s offerings. Nestled in a secluded neighborhood in San Carlos, Pangisinan, the property features a natural spring lake for fishing, paddle boarding, kayaking, dip pool and/or relaxing on the lake’s pontoon boat.

The Space

The Mariposa Farm Resort is a farm with 4 / 1-bedroom NIPA HUTS and a 6 room hotel. The Street Level 1 room is 1 of the 2 rooms on the street level of the hotel, it has a queen bed and trundle and can sleep 3 people. There are 4 rooms on the lake level of the hotel. It has a twin bunk bed and trundle and sleeps 3 people. Each room has its own bathroom with water heater and bidet. It also has air conditioner and closet for clothes. It also has a private patio with chairs and table with the view of the lake. The compound has bamboo, mango trees, cacao tree, etc. The natural spring lake has shrimps, tilapia, kuhol, etc. It has small pool for dipping, and outdoor grill, 2 outdoor enclosed bathroom, and outside kitchen. There are hummocks, karaoke machine, paddle board, kayak and pontoon you can reserve for use and take it in the water for relaxation and have lunch

Guest Access

Guest have access to the lake, paddle board, kayak and pontoon for free with reservation. There is an outside kitchen with refrigerator that can be used to store food. There is stove where guest can cook for a small fee of P100 per day. There is also fishing rods available, fishes caught will be priced by the kilo below market price. There is also 2 outside bathroom with shower to use for rinsing after swimming from the lake or removing the smell of a freshly caught fish. For entertainment, you can rent a karaoke machine.

Other Things To Note

TOWELS and toiletries will not be provided. Please note valid photo ID will be required after booking. Property staff is instructed to not open gate unless ID is verified.
