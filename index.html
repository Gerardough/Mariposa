Where Serenity Blossoms Amidst Nature's Embrace

Description

The Mariposa Farm Resort is a tranquil farm, family oriented and peaceful hotel like property that boasts 6 spacious rooms for guests to get away from the hustle and bustle of the city and embrace nature and all of its offerings. Nestled in a secluded neighborhood in San Carlos, Pangasinan, the property features a natural spring lake for fishing, paddle boarding , and/or kayaking. A dip pool and a pontoon boat are available for relaxing while on the property.

Overview

Mariposa Farm Resort is the latest property under the management of Villamor Properties. Based in Southern California, Villamor Properties has long term and short term rental properties in Southern California, USA, Metro Manila and it’s premier resort property, Mariposa Farm Resort located in San Carlos, Pangasinan, Philippines.

Mariposa Farm Resort was developed to be a short term rental property that provides Guests with an environment that is conducive to leisure, relaxation, engaging with nature and enjoying various water sports on the natural spring lake located in the center of the property. It boasts a six bedroom, Apartment style building which overlooks the lake. In addition, there are 4 Bahay Kubo Huts for guest who like to enjoy the natural outdoor surroundings up close. The property has Acacia trees, native Bamboo and numerous native fruit trees which includes Mango, Guyobano, Avocado, Calamansi, Cacao and Banana, to name a few.

Following the guidelines of a true farm, there will be various livestock that will be a part of the resort experience for young and old. Plans are underway for Goats, Chickens, Geese, and Native Pigs to be housed on the property in order of provide a true farm experience when visiting the property.

In addition, an organic Garden is under development with the ultimate goal of providing fresh herbs and vegetables from “Farm to Table” while Guest are visiting the property.
