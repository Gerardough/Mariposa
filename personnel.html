Executive Members

Sherrie Villamor

Founder and Visionary

Sherrie Villamor grew up in Caloocan City and attended Our Lady of Grace Academy (OLGA). She is is a graduate of Mapua Institute of Technology, Philippines with a double degree in Electrical Engineering and Electrical Communications. After migrating to the US, Ms. Villamor excelled as a Program Manager and Supplier Program Manager for the world’s largest aerospace “Fortune 5” companies to include the Boeing Company and Raytheon Technologies. She has received numerous corporate awards for exemplary performance and support to include the prestigious Presidential Award from Raytheon Technologies in 20XX. While at The Boeing Company, Ms Villamor was the Senior International Supplier Program Manager for commercial Satellite programs and was well respected by her colleagues and international customers. She retired from the Aerospace industry in 2020 to devote her efforts full time to grow Villamor properties to an international entity as it is today.

Cliff Johnson is the Husband of the company founder, Sherrie Villamor, thus completing the family owned business venture. Mr Johnson is a graduate of California State University Fullerton with a degree in Electrical Engineering with a special emphasis of Electronics Communication. He is also a graduate of the Glen Anderson School of Business, University Of California Los Angeles (UCLA) completing all requirements for an Executive Management Certification/Degree. Mr. Johnson excelled as a Test Engineer and Field Service Engineer for High Frequency Radio systems and Tactical Advance Computers for Raytheon Technologies, one of the world’s largest Aerospace Companies. Mr Johnson soon transitioned to International Marketing and Business Development for Air Defense Radar Systems and due to his success, he was ultimate promoted to International Director of Business Development for Asia, the Middle East, and Europe. In 2020, Mr. Johnson retired from Aerospace to join his wife in promoting Villamor Properties in the US domestic marketplace (Central/Souther California) and the international marketplace (the Philippines).

Company Vice President of Marketing and Business Development Strategy

Alexander Gasper

Company President

Alexander Gasper is the son of founder Sherrie Villamor. He is a 2016 graduate from California Polytechnic State University San Luis Obispo, California which is ranked in the top 1% for US Colleges of Engineering and is ranked #1 for Most Innovative Schools. Mr. Gasper has completed and passed the National Council of Examiners for Engineering and Surveying (NCEES) Fundamentals of Engineering Exam and is presently working towards his Profession Engineering (PE) certification. His emphasis is focused on “Green Energy” and is presently a Senior Design Manager at one of the US’ emerging Solar Power Companies. This knowledge and background in the solar industry will be instrumental in the migration of Mariposa Farm Resort to a Solar Powered/Green Energy facility in the future. In addition, Mr. Gasper is presently the Property Manager for all of the Villamor Properties in the US and the Philippines. He manages the short term and long term rental contracts and coordinates all maintenance and repairs for all Villamor properties worldwide.

Mariposa Farm Resort

Mariposa Farm Resort is the latest property under the management of Villamor Properties. Based in Southern California, Villamor Properties has long term and short term rental properties in Southern California, USA, Metro Manila and it’s premier resort property, Mariposa Farm Resort located in San Carlos, Pangasinan, Philippines.
